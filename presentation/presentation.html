Solving the "Team Screen" Trap

The Problem

Fetching only "Today's" games leaves the database with no history, making the Team Detail screen empty.

The Solution

Denormalization. The backend fetches a 7-day backfill and pre-calculates the history into a teams collection.

The Result

Instant load times. The mobile app performs 1 simple read instead of complex, expensive queries.
